Power up your STEM Outreach with Megawatt!

Why partner with Megawatt?

  1. 1 Demonstrate your commitment to STEM to customers, employees, your local community and wider industry.
  2. 2 Secure your future workforce by inspiring the next generation of engineers and scientists to deliver on net zero.
  3. 3 Help teachers link the school curriculum to careers and opportunities in your organisation and wider industry.

The Megawatt offer

We offer a number of different engagement activities that can be adapted to suit your organisations needs. These range from individual school visits to full bespoke outreach programmes:

School Visits

Sponsor the Megawatt team to visit schools in your local area. They will deliver a Megawatt session, a careers talk with a focus on the energy industry, train teachers to use Megawatt and provide them with their own set of Megawatt.

Train STEM Ambassadors from your organisation to use Megawatt in schools. Provide them with the resources they need to deliver a fantastic classroom activity using Megawatt whilst sharing their experiences working in your organisation.

STEM Ambassador Training

Teacher training

Across all our activities, we offer branding opportunities on Megawatt and on our website. Further bespoke offerings are available for your organisation to promote your STEM Outreach activities.

Be like Magnox!

Magnox Ltd recently relaunched their STEM Outreach programme by partnering with Megawatt. We trained STEM Ambassadors across 3 sites to use Megawatt, and we have over 15 school sessions scheduled to take place before the end of 2022.

This provides Magnox with a fantastic opportunity to connect with their local schools, engaging students on the topic of energy and climate change while sharing the important work they do decommissioning the UK’s nuclear power plants.
